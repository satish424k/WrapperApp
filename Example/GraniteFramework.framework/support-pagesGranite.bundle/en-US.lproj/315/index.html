How can I tell how many heat stages I have?

The Lyric Thermostat is compatible with single stage and multi stage heating systems. If you only connected a wire to the W terminal on your Lyric thermostat you have a single stage heating system and should select 1. If you connected a wire to W and W2 you have a two stage heating system and should select 2.
