Do I need to go into Advanced Configuration to install my Lyric Thermostat?

The Lyric Thermostat comes set to control the most common system types meaning you likely do not need to go into the Advanced Configuration. However, since Lyric is designed to work with a wide variety of heating and cooling systems as well as indoor air quality products such as whole house humidifiers, whole house dehumidifiers, and ventilators the Advanced Configuration menu allows you more options when setting up your equipment
